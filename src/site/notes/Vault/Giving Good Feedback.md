1. The micro-yes question
“Do you have a few minutes to talk about this?”
2. Give a data point
Give specific examples of something
Don’t use blur words.
Say exactly what they did and why it was good or bad
3. Impact statement
Say exactly how that data point impacted you
4. Question
“This is what I’m thinking, what are your thoughts?” Etc

Ask for feedback regularly. Don’t wait for push feedback ask for pull feedback.
